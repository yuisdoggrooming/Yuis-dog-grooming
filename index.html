Welcome to Our Quiet Retreat

At Yui's Dog Grooming, we believe every dog deserves a spa day tailored to their unique needs. Operating exclusively as a private, cage-free home salon, we focus entirely on one dog at a time. This quiet, gentle approach ensures a completely stress-free environment, making it especially beneficial for senior dogs or anxious pups who might feel overwhelmed in a busy, loud commercial studio. Your furry best friend will receive undivided attention and top-tier care.

Meet Your Groomer

Hi, I'm Yui!

I am a certified professional dog groomer with over 15 years of industry experience. I am deeply passionate about providing high-quality care with a focus on gentle, low-stress handling.

I specifically designed my private, home-based studio to be a completely calming and relaxing retreat for your pets. By focusing entirely on one dog at a time, I ensure a quiet, positive experience for pups who might feel overwhelmed in a busy commercial environment.

When I'm not in the salon, you can usually find me baking treats in the kitchen or playing with my two kids and our sweet 9-year-old rescue Border Collie mix, Lily!
