Welcome to Our Quiet Retreat

At Yui's Dog Grooming, we believe every dog deserves a spa day tailored to their unique needs. Operating exclusively as a private, cage-free home salon, we focus entirely on one dog at a time. This quiet, gentle approach ensures a completely stress-free environment, making it especially beneficial for senior dogs or anxious pups who might feel overwhelmed in a busy, loud commercial studio. Your furry best friend will receive undivided attention and top-tier care.

Meet Your Groomer

Hi, I'm Yui!
